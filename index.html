About Me

Hey, I'm Vishwajit — an Associate Product Manager based out of Mumbai, India.

I started my career in pharmaceutical marketing at Lupin, where I spent over 11 months decoding the needs of doctors, patients, and market segments in one of the most regulated industries in the world.

But curiosity kept pulling me toward something bigger. I wanted to build things — not just market them. That's when I made a deliberate pivot into Product Management through Airtribe's rigorous PM cohort...

Today, I blend the structured thinking of pharma with the velocity of AI-native tooling — using ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, NotebookLM, and more to research, prototype, and ship fast.
