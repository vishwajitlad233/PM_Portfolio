Critical Insights

Restaurant Browse → Cart: −24pp

The biggest leak. Users scroll menus but don't add. Root causes: decision paralysis from unstructured menus and missing social signals.

Fix: AI-driven menu highlights + "People like you ordered" layer.

Checkout → Order Placed: −7pp

Payment friction: UPI timeouts and coupon-not-applied rage taps. 39% of payment failures are UPI timeout-related.

Fix: Silent UPI retry flow, 1-tap re-order for frequent combos.

The M3 Cliff Insight

31% of non-Gold users churn between M2 and M3. This is the "novelty wear-off" phase. The window between M1 and M3 is when Gold trial conversion must happen. If we secure a Gold subscription before M3, that single user's 6-month order volume effectively triples.
